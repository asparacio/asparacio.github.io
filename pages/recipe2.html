Tiramisu

Mascarpone custard layered with whipped cream and rum and coffee soaked ladyfingers.

Before You Bake Info

  • Bake time: ~5 hours
  • Makes 12 servings
  • 387 calories per serving

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup strong brewed coffee, room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.
    1. Boil gently for 1 minute, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
    2. Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator 1 hour.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.
    1. Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth.
  3. In a small bowl, combine coffee and rum.
    1. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with coffee mixture.
  4. Arrange half of soaked ladyfingers in bottom of 7 by 11 inch dish.
    1. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then half of whipped cream over that.
    2. Repeat layers and sprinkle with cocoa.
    3. Cover and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, until set.

Footnotes

  • Tip
    • Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier.
